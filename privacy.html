Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

ClickKing is built to be a privacy-first tool. We aim to collect the absolute minimum data required to show you how your links are performing. We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. Data We Collect (As a Controller)

When you sign up as a customer, we collect your Email address for account management and Billing information, which is processed securely by our Merchant of Record. We do not store full credit card details on our servers.

3. Tracking Data (As a Processor)

When someone clicks a ClickKing link, we process:

  • IP Address: Processed momentarily to determine approximate location (Country/City) and then anonymized.
  • Referrer & User Agent: To show you where your traffic originates and what devices are used.

Cookies: ClickKing does not use tracking cookies for visitors clicking your links.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@metallicode.com to exercise these rights.
